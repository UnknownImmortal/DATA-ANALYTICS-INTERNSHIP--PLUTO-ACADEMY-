##  Repository Structure & Project Directory

The deliverables in this repository are divided into two dedicated project directories:

###  [Project 01: E-Commerce Sales Performance Analysis](./Project_01_Ecommerce_Analysis/)
- **Objective:** Acted as an in-house Data Analyst to clean, merge, and evaluate 12 months of transactional sales records from the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset.
- **Key Deliverables:** - Automated data cleaning and relational table merging.
  - A 1-page interactive executive tracking dashboard.
  - 5 comprehensive, data-backed business insights and strategic optimization recommendations.
- **Core Insights Snippet:** Discovered that low-ticket items unexpectedly suffered from high 1-star reviews due to low-priority logistics tracking rather than product material quality defects.

###  [Project 02: Student Performance Analysis](./Project_02_Student_Performance/)
- **Objective:** Processed 1,000 academic student records to evaluate demographic, socioeconomic, and preparatory drivers behind test scores for an academic institution.
- **Key Deliverables:**
  - 5-line structural data summary profile and exploratory data analysis (EDA).
  - At-risk student segmentation mapping (identifying students scoring $< 50$ in any core subject).
  - A formal Principal's Report outlining 5 core findings and 3 administrative interventions.
- **Core Insights Snippet:** Students who bypassed test preparation courses experienced an academic failure risk nearly three times higher than those who completed them.
